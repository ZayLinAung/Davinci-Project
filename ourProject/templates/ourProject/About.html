Who We Are

We are the Brunelleschi Group. We are a group of 4 passionate first-year developer. We named ourselves after Brunelleschi because he is renowned architech, who is known for his innovations. We strive to innovate like Brunelleschi and make the world a better place through our skills and new technology.

Our Goals

As first years, we have seen others struggle to meet up with other first years. Thus, we have decided to create an web application where fellow first year, incoming students, and upper years can match with students of the same year. By doing so, we strive to create a more inclusive community where there are no students left out.

What We Do

Over a span of 2 weeks, we have collaborated to create a web application where fellow students and match with their peers of the same year. The students will sign up and fill out a form containing information about where they go to school and what they are interested in. Based on their input, we match them with a person with a similar profile.

The Process

For this web application, we have used Python and  Django for the backend, and Nicepages for the frontend. Over 2 weeks, we have tirelessly worked on this project, facing challenges and overcoming them together. Through this project, we have developed our communication skills and developed our technical skils.

Our Future

We have a big vision for this web application. In the future, we want to add many other schools and universities as we want every university to have an inclusive community. We wish to also add a chat system where first years can connect with other first years and a system where students can post about study group session.
